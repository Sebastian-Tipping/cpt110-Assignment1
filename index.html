Project Idea

overview

A security RFID card which you would use to replace or make passwords more secure or to encrypt files on your computer or device.
It would work like a myki or similar with a scanner built into your computer/phone or whatever else you want to use.
So you would just scan your card it would take that signal and then unlock a folder or log you into an application or website either skipping a password or working like a 2-factor authenticator but without requiring you to write the code down making it quicker and easier for use.

Motivation

People don't like complicated passwords so they make them simple and easy to remember, which is problematic because they are then easy to hack.
One option is to write your password down somewhere (I know a lot of people write them on sticky note they put on their monitor) or to use a password manager which works till you forget the password to that or someone else gets Into it in which case you may as well have no password at all.
This could replace the need for passwords as an offsite key that is more secure and easier to remember than a long password

Description

The device would have 2 different parts. The first part which would be the card which would have an RFID chip inside of it. The RFID chip would be unique to the user, which would be scanned on the back of your phone, on your laptop, etc.

The reason for these cards is they are relatively to manufacture and program with the right tools, while they are small enough to fit in your wallet easily making them harder to lose than a separate object.

The second part would be the scanners on your devices. This scanner could be built into your phone, laptop or computer alternatively it could be built as a stand-alone device that could be plugged in via USB to make it compatible with older technology,
The scanner would send out a pulse then receive an input from the chip in the card then pass it through a hashing and salting process followed by checking it to a database to see if it matches a master key also unique to the user showing that it is the right card.

This would make a system which is portable because all you would really need is the card and a device which can read the card ideally your computer or phone which you plan to access the data on. Meaning if you were to be working on some sensitive files at while at home (If they were uploaded to the cloud) and wanted to continue working on them while out or at work, you could unlock the files just by tapping your card on your computer.

You could also make multiple cards which have the same key for multiple users working on the same project, so companies who work on very important information could get a set of cards for one project and hand them out to only the people allowed to access the data.

It would also be easy to give someone the account or the file just by giving them the physical card like if you want to sell an account you need to change all the details or worry about the original user getting back on or if you want to share an account you need to change the password to something you tell them then change it back after you want to stop sharing the account. With this system, you could just give them the card for a week then when you get the card back you know for sure they can not get back in without a lot of effort

Then the device would unlock the said folder or application with no more than just a quick tap from the user making the need for a complicated password less necessary. Making easier and quicker for the user while being more secure than a basic password.

Tools and Technologies

You would require the means to make or obtain both the Cards with the RFID chips as well as the receivers.

Working with other companies to get the receivers built into their products would be helpful so people would not need the standalone receivers making the package that you need to carry smaller and making the use quicker and easier

you would also need to program the chips with a random set of characters which is encrypted and then uploaded to a database that you would need to host as well as designing and developing an app that the user would install or have prebuilt into the device to take the data from the RFID chip, verify then unlock or login to whatever you are trying to access.

Skill required

Some of the skills required would involve knowledge of making the RFID cards and how to program them.

Someone would also need to know how to design, prototype and then build the receivers, and the housing for the stand-alone models
It would also be helpful to have someone work with other companies for their products to have the receivers

It would require knowledge of designing and managing a database to store the binary codes the cards have programmed to them.

Another thing that would be required is someone who can design the program that takes the signal, checks it by comparing it to the database and then unlocking the front end for the user.

Outcome

Should this work than long and complicated passwords that can be forgotten will be less necessary and accounts will be more secure.
Logging into your account or trying to access private data will be quicker and easier as just one tap will log you in.
Also since there will be no need to enter a password you will also be safer from things from attacks like keylogging making your accounts more secure from hacking.

So with this working out Cybersecurity will be increased but the ease of access into your accounts will be increased as well.
